特效技能: Totem Of Undying
--------------------------

于技能目标位置生成播放激活不死图腾效果.

可修改model以修改显示的粒子贴图.

无法关闭音效.

示例
-----

```yaml
 Skills:
 - totemofundying{model=2} @Self
```

额外信息
---

- 别称: effect:totemresurrection, totemeffect, totemresurrection, e:totemresurrection, totemresurrectioneffect, totemofundying, e:totemofundying, totemeffect
